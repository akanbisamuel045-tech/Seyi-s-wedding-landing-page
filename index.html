Our Love Story

How We Met

It was a beautiful spring day in 2019 when our paths first crossed at a mutual friend's art gallery opening. Seyi was admiring a painting, and Tunmise accidentally bumped into him while taking a step back to get a better view. They both laughed, and what started as an awkward moment turned into hours of conversation about art, life, and dreams.

The First Date

Three days later, Seyi gathered the courage to ask Tunmise out for coffee. That coffee date turned into a walk through the park, which turned into dinner, which turned into the beginning of forever. They talked until the stars came out, realizing they had found something truly special.

The Proposal

On their five-year anniversary, Seyi took Tunmise back to the art gallery where they first met. The gallery owner had arranged for them to have the space to themselves. As Tunmise turned to look at the same painting from that fateful day, she found Seyi on one knee. Through happy tears, she said yes!
